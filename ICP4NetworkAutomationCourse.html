ZN777: Cloud Pak for Network Automation 2.1 Administration and Configuration

The first portion of the ZN777 course, and the course prerequisite, is DL10032G, a free course without labs that focuses on the fundamental principles, architecture, and terminology of the Cloud Pak for Network Automation.

The ZN777 self-paced with lab course teaches the skills that are needed to successfully install, configure, and use Cloud Pak for Network Automation (CP4NA). This course is the second part of a self-paced training offering for Cloud Pak for Network Automation and focuses on hands-on lab exercises to describe, deploy, and manage VOIP and telco components.

ZN777 Course Outline

About this course:

The free prerequisite for this course is DL10032G. It is expected that the DL10032G course is completed before starting the ZN777 course.

Students need to pay to attend the ZN777 course. Please see the instructions on how to enroll in the course in the description for the ZN777 course.

The course is guaranteed to run on the dates below as long as there is at least one paying student enrolled. All students must pay to attend this course.

The ZN777 course is a self-paced course. The lectures are recorded and there are labs. The course will take two full days to complete but you are given three full days to complete the lectures and labs and provide time to work with the software.

Be certain you allow sufficient time to complete the course as there are no time extensions to use the lab environment as it is very expensive to host.
